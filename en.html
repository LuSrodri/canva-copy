How to use?

Just follow the steps and see how easy it is to remove image backgrounds:

  1. Go to I Hate Background

  2. Select an image

  3. Wait a few seconds

  4. Done!

Now enjoy your image with a transparent background

How does it work?

I Hate Background uses advanced image segmentation techniques to detect and remove the background directly in your browser. That means: no image is uploaded to any external server, ensuring your privacy and security. This way the process is fast, efficient, and completely free. Learn more about the technology for running AI models locally.

💡Tip: You can also remove backgrounds from multiple images at the same time. Drag and drop all of them and get the files ready to use.
