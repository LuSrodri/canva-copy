How to use?

  2. Select an image

  4. Done!

How does it work?

I Hate Background uses advanced image segmentation techniques to detect and remove the background directly in your browser. That means: no image is uploaded to any external server, ensuring your privacy and security. This way the process is fast, efficient, and completely free.
